About Us

The staff team on Simple State RP is one of the best out there. From having custom ped and cars to a full permission-based admin menu. The staff team is a group of exceptional roleplayers that come together to moderate the server to make it a fun and amzing place for you to play. If you can handle stressful situations and make quick decisions on the fly the staff is meant for you! 

Our Guidelines

+ Staff must be respectful at all times and must never lose their temper.
+ Staff must remain professional in stressful situations.
+ Staff must never hesitate to respond to a report.
+ Staff must never show off their powers.
+ Staff must remain unbiased in every situation
+ Staff must treat everyone equally to all members.
